our story

Frank Hsu

i love to cook, fish, and be barefoot

Leslie Lim

i love to bake, exercise, and wander

we met at church sometime ago in college

i was a freshman i was a sophmore

although we both attended nyu and the same small church, even served together in ministry - but we never really crossed paths or even spoke more than a few words each time

there no time to waste. career was what mattered to me. mind on mah money, money on mah mind i studied abroad, made close friends, and did all that i wanted in college
and did what i wanted in college

fast forward a few years, i move to north carolina to teach i move to san francisco for a change of career

then we meet again at a friend's wedding, im still single, but saw leslie and pulled out all the moves, invited a bunch of them to visit me, etc. yet she did not want to dance.. the wedding was fun

leslie actually comes along to sf a few months later and ends up staying with frank. THIS IS MY SHOT frank asks if he could hitch a ride down to la with me

a 6 hour drive together should be enough to woo her we arrive in la and said our final goodbyes over a meal at cafe gratitude my favorite meal ever probably the worst meal i've ever had, this could be a deal breaker

my snapchat game is strong why is he talking to me so much snapchat becomes texting, texting becomes calling, calling becomes movie over skype.

chinese new years approaches i invited leslie and friends for hotpot at my house, probably the biggest hint ever to my parents and friends. i have nothing else to do in the days following i asked her out for dinner date
