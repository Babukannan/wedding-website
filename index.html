our story

Frank Hsu

i love cooking, fishing, and running barefoot

Leslie Lim

i love baking, exercising, and healthy things

we met at church sometime ago in college

i was a freshman    i was a sophmore

although we both attended nyu, the same small church, and even served together in ministry

for one reason or another, we never really spoke more than a few words to each other

and fast forward a couple of years

my career takes me to san francisco for a start-up    im back in nyc finishing my second year of law school

we eventually cross paths again in nyc at a friend's wedding

come visit me in sf, i said jokingly    maybe, i'll think about it (not really)

a few short months later

somehow im driving up to SF and staying with frank    He makes all things work together for our good

sparks begin to fly... but a 6 hour drive down to LA and a goodbye dinner later - we just say goodbye

but enter frank's snapchat and texting game

(along with making of a murderer and other things frank thinks leslie likes)

some more time passes and frank returns home for chinese new years

i invite leslie to my house for hotpot all gatsby-like    i have nothing else to do

our first, second, and third dates all happened that week

these were amazingly fully planned hour-by-hour best of nyc day-long dates    kidnappings

then snapchat becomes texting, texting becomes calling, and calling into facetime + movie nights

and we made the best of long distance

now we're ready to start our lives together
